Data We Retain (Legal Requirements)

The following data may be retained for legal and regulatory purposes:

  • Financial Records: Payment transactions may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Legal Compliance: Data related to ongoing legal proceedings or investigations
  • Security Logs: Access logs and security-related data for up to 90 days
  • Backup Data: Encrypted backup data may be retained for up to 30 days

What Happens After Deletion

Immediate Effects:

  • Your account will be permanently deactivated
  • You will lose access to all app features
  • All personal data will be removed from our active systems
  • You will no longer receive notifications or communications

Processing Time:

  • Account Deactivation: Immediate
  • Data Deletion: Within 30 days
  • Backup Cleanup: Within 60 days
  • Confirmation Email: Sent within 7 days

Recreating Your Account

If you change your mind after deletion, you can create a new account using a different email address. However, please note that:

  • You cannot recover your previous data
  • You will need to provide all information again
  • Your previous booking history and preferences will not be restored
  • You may need to re-verify your identity
